\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Fast and Safe planner with SDF-\/guided costs and Lazy Theta$\ast$. 

Enhances A$\ast$ by\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Using a signed-\/distance-\/like map (via EDT on free space) to penalize traversal near obstacles,
\item Projecting neighbor expansion toward a blended target direction formed by goal direction and safest local direction,
\item Attempting direct LOS connections to the parent\textquotesingle{}s parent (Lazy Theta$\ast$) to reduce expansions and path zig-\/zags. 
\end{DoxyItemize}
